Match‑hub is a web‑based platform designed to help researchers automatically identify relevant on‑call funding opportunities.

Researchers upload their areas of expertise and their most recent CVs to Match‑hub, where the information is processed and analyzed by AI. The extracted data is then consolidated and displayed in the Expert Database.

Match‑hub continuously monitors and updates funding calls from Norwegian and EU funding bodies. The AI engine highlights key requirements, eligibility criteria, and important keywords for each call.

The Matching Engine automatically aligns the requirements of each funding call with the registered experts. It presents a match percentage along with supporting evidence to illustrate how well an expert fits the call
